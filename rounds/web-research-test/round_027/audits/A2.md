### P1. Fourth-moment expansion mishandles complex weights

A2 explicitly says the two-sided convention allows complex dyadic weights `w_D(d)`, but the displayed expansion of `|S_2|^4` uses

$$
\prod_{j=1}^4 w_D(d_j)
$$

instead of the conjugated pattern required by

$$
S_2\overline{S_2}S_2\overline{S_2}.
$$

For complex weights, the coefficient should be

$$
w_D(d_1)\overline{w_D(d_2)}w_D(d_3)\overline{w_D(d_4)}.
$$

As written, the fourth-moment formula is valid only under an unstated real-weight assumption. This also affects any later signed-mass or exact-bin formula copied from that expansion.

Recommended correction: either add `w_D` real-valued as a standing hypothesis for the Round 27 finite statistics, or correct all fourth-moment and kernel formulas to use conjugated weights.
